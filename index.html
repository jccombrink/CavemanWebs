Caveblend*

After 16 years as part of the Specialized Global Racing Team, Conrad "The Caveman" Stoltz has finally chosen caffeine as a worthy successor to the lactic acid pumping through his veins.

Swim, bike, run has evolved into STOLTZ Racing, STOLTZ Trails and Caveman Cafe. Although once in a blue moon, you might even get lucky and find your post-ride espresso being brewed by The Caveman himself.

From the mythical trails of Jonkershoek and Coetzenburg to the infamous "G-Spot" and every single square milimeter in between. The love affair with Stellenbosch continues. At the heart of it all lies the new game-changing Specialized Stellenbosch ; Nirvana to cyclists, and now also the untamed home to Caveman Cafe.
